Scene 1: The Coffee Shop - Morning

Alex, a tall young man with dark hair and glasses, sits at a small table in the corner of a cozy coffee shop. He's wearing a blue hoodie and jeans, with a laptop open in front of him. The morning sun streams through the large windows, creating bright natural lighting. The atmosphere is peaceful and productive. A steaming cup of coffee sits next to his laptop, and his backpack rests on the floor beside his chair.

Scene 2: The Coffee Shop - Morning (Later)

Alex is still at the same table, still wearing his blue hoodie and glasses. He's now typing furiously on his laptop, with the same coffee cup nearby. The lighting remains bright and natural from the morning sun. The peaceful atmosphere continues, though Alex appears more focused and intense in his work.

Scene 3: The Coffee Shop - Afternoon

Alex remains at his corner table, maintaining his consistent appearance with the blue hoodie and glasses. The laptop is still open, but now he's leaning back in his chair, looking thoughtful. The lighting has shifted to softer afternoon light, creating a warm, comfortable atmosphere. His backpack is still on the floor, and he's ordered a fresh cup of coffee.

Scene 4: The Coffee Shop - Evening

As evening approaches, Alex is still at his familiar corner table, wearing the same blue hoodie and glasses. He's now packing up his laptop into his backpack. The lighting has become dimmer and more artificial as the natural light fades. The atmosphere shifts to a cozy, intimate mood. The coffee shop is quieter now, with fewer customers.

Scene 5: The Coffee Shop - Night

Alex returns to the coffee shop at night, still wearing his signature blue hoodie and glasses. He sits at the same corner table, but now the shop has dramatic evening lighting with warm artificial lights creating shadows and highlights. The atmosphere is mysterious and contemplative. He's brought his laptop again and ordered another coffee, maintaining his routine.

Scene 6: The Coffee Shop - Next Morning

The next morning, Alex is back at his usual corner table, wearing the same blue hoodie and glasses. The morning sun streams through the windows again, creating the same bright natural lighting as the first day. The peaceful, productive atmosphere has returned. His laptop is open, coffee cup nearby, and backpack on the floor - everything exactly as it was before, showing the consistency of his routine and the coffee shop's unchanging environment.
